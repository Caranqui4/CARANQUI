Hello! I'm Michael Caranqui, a freelance artist with a degree in Plastic Arts from the Central University of Ecuador. My artistic work focuses on exploring the boundaries between traditional and digital techniques in art. My career as a freelance artist has allowed me to experiment with a wide variety of mediums and styles, from oil painting and traditional drawing to digital illustration. I'm passionate about the interaction between traditional and modern techniques, and how these techniques can coexist and complement each other in a work of art.
